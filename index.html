Before you upload your data

  • Your file never leaves your browser. It is read locally using the FileReader API. Nothing is sent to any server.
  • Historical analysis only. Win rates and rankings are computed from past signals and do not predict future performance.
  • No investment advice. Nothing on this dashboard should influence any buy, sell, or hold decision.
  • Accuracy depends on your data. Results are only as reliable as the signals your evaluate.py run parsed and evaluated.
  • No affiliation. SignalTrail is not affiliated with SEBI, NSE, BSE, or any brokerage.
  • You are responsible for ensuring the channels you evaluated are ones you have legal access to.

Analyze your own channels

Run the evaluation scripts on your machine, then drop the output file here. Your Telegram credentials never leave your device.

  1. Download scripts Unzip — no git required
  2. pip install -r requirements.txt
  3. python3 scripts/login.py — one-time Telegram auth
  4. PYTHONPATH=. python3 scripts/evaluate.py — generates leaderboard-public.json

What the metrics mean Plain-English guide to the public v2-style snapshot Open to read

Messages scanned

Total Telegram messages ingested in the backfill window before parsing.

Parsed calls

Messages the parser could turn into actionable stock calls or setups.

Outcome rows

All evaluated result rows, including multiple horizons and method types.

Call win %

Share of calls that ended as wins at the call level after resolution.

Resolved win %

Win rate after excluding unresolved or incomplete cases from the denominator.

Row win %

Win rate across every evaluated row, including horizon-based rows.

Target/stop win %

How often a full trade plan hit target before stop when both levels were available.

Directional win %

Directional edge against the benchmark when the parser had to fall back to horizon-based evaluation.

Bayes win %

Shrinkage-adjusted win rate that softens small-sample spikes.

Avg R

Average return in R-multiples; positive means the sample made money on average.

Profit factor

Gross wins divided by gross losses; above 1.0 is better, higher is stronger.

Confidence

Sample-size label that tells you how much trust to place in the row.
