Core Rules

SignalTrail evaluates historical outcomes of public Telegram calls. It does not provide personalized recommendations.

  • Trade-plan calls use target/stop simulation.
  • Incomplete calls use directional horizon fallback.
  • Metrics are split by call-level, row-level, and resolved-only denominators.
  • Minimum sample thresholds determine confidence tier.

Important Limits

  • Historical performance does not predict future returns.
  • Parser ambiguity may create errors.
  • Private follow-up instructions are not observable.
